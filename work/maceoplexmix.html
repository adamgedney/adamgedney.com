Maceo Plex Fansite

Maceoplexmix.com

A fansite hosting DJ mixes for an international electronic music DJ

Project Description

Maceoplexmix.com is a simple long-scrolling website designed to be a resource for fans of Maceop Plex. Fans will find a large variety of DJ mixes linked to from the website. The website was built light for easy mobile or desktop usability.

https://github.com/adamgedney/maceoplexmix.com
