Simple Picasa Slideshow

Simple Picasa Slideshow

A JQuery plugin for displaying a slideshow from a Picasa album

Project Description

Simple Picasa Slideshow was developed from need, as most things are. When building simple client brochure sites, I needed a way to let them manage their image content without having to call me every time they wanted to swap out an image form their slideshow. Simple Picasa Slideshow was born! Built on JQuery and Lightbox, my open source plugin is lightweight, and super simple to implement.

https://github.com/adamgedney/simplepicasaslideshow.com
